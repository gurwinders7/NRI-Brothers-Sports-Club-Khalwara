About Us

Welcome to the NRI Brothers Sports Club Khalwara! Our club has been a vibrant hub for sports enthusiasts of all ages and skill levels in the Khalwara region. We are a community-driven organization dedicated to promoting sportsmanship, camaraderie, and the pursuit of excellence in various sports disciplines.

Our Sports

  • Football

Our Vision:

At NRI Brothers Sports Club Khalwara, our vision is to create an inclusive and empowering environment that fosters a passion for sports and physical fitness. We strive to nurture talent, inspire teamwork, and instill valuable life skills through the power of sports. Our ultimate goal is to make a positive impact on the lives of our members and the community as a whole.

Mission

Our mission is to provide exceptional sports facilities, professional coaching, and a wide range of sporting activities to our members. We aim to be the go-to destination for sports enthusiasts, where they can not only hone their skills but also build lasting friendships and unforgettable memories.
